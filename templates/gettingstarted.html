What is Impact 2030?


Impact 2030 is a collaboration between the private sector, United Nations, civil society, academia, and
philanthropic organization to end poverty, combat climate change and fight injustice and inequality by 2030. Each
Impact 2030 founding partner has commited to strategically aligningand mobilizing employee actions towards
one or more of the SDG's (Sustainable Development Goals).
By completing this profile, your organization will be contributing to the global fight to bring about sustainable
societal change and make a real difference. As more organizations become invloved, we can produce reports to
show you a profile of your personal impact. This partnership between our corporate partners and your
organization is crucial to the success of this UN initiative and the improvement of the impact measurement.
